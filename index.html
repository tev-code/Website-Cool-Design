The Kaas Cpt brand isn't just about smoking weed and getting high. We are a conscious brand. It's a lifestyle, a change of thinking, crossed with easy-going/chillaxed Cape Town culture, crossed with marijuana culture,crossed with fighting towards the rising tide of conformity.All of this rolled into one joint, forms Kaas CPT.

KaasCPT First Came About When Myself And A Friend, Who Also Has A Passion For Plants And Gardening, Started Talking About Having A Marijuana Farm When The Laws Eventually Fall Into Place. I’ve Always Had A Passion For Art And Design, And Have Always Wanted To Wear Clothing With My Own Designs On Them, And That’s How This Cookie Crumbled.
